Watch For

Aggression & Attack

  • → Attack vs. settle
  • → Contact at the rim
  • → Drives with purpose

Rebounding & Effort

  • → Box out first
  • → Go for the rebound
  • → Hustle on loose balls

Defense

  • → Low stance
  • → Ball pressure
  • → Help & rotate

Passing & IQ

  • → Smart pass vs. forced shot
  • → Quick decisions

Off-Ball

  • → Cuts & screens
  • → Works to get open

Leadership

  • → Next-play mentality
  • → Talks & brings energy

Post-Game Reflection

1. What's the #1 thing your player did that you want to add to YOUR game?
2. What effort play stood out most tonight — from anyone on the court?
3. What did the best players do that most players on the court didn't?
