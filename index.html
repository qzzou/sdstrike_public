Our Mission

Most school-team players don't play club basketball — whether it's the cost, the time commitment, or the travel-heavy schedule. So they go 7 months with nothing.

SDStrike fills the gap with quality coaching, competitive games, and a community of players who want to get better. Affordable. Local. Development-focused. For every school-team player in San Diego.
